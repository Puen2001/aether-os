# Roadmap

AETHER OS is free and works today. This is where it's headed. It's also honest about
being an early, solo-built project — the order and timing will follow what people actually
ask for.

## The north star — any medium, one core

AETHER OS is not an app; it's a personal AI **core** with pluggable front-ends. Your memory,
your vaults, and your agents live in the core. The way you reach them — voice, a messaging
app (Telegram/Discord), mobile, desktop, the web — is just a *terminal* that plugs in. Swap
the terminal; the core and its memory of you stay the same. Reaching your AETHER from any
medium is a **core, free principle** (specific polished bridges may land in Pro; the ability
to connect is not paywalled).

- A Telegram bridge stub already ships in `tools/`.
- Next: reference connectors for voice and one more chat medium.

## Local-LLM mode

Run AETHER fully on local/open models — offline-capable, provider-independent, maximum
privacy. Today AETHER runs on Claude Code; this severs the hard dependency on any single
cloud provider.

## Domain packs

Drop-in bundles of role agents + starter vaults for a specific life domain, so you don't
start from a blank page:

- a **personal trainer** / health coach
- a finance coach
- a study tutor

## Beyond the free tier

A future **AETHER Cloud / Pro** would add managed convenience on top of the free scaffold —
hosted setup, automated multi-agent orchestration, managed multi-machine sync,
client-showable audit/attestation artifacts, and premium skill/domain packs. The free tier
stays complete and useful on its own, forever.
